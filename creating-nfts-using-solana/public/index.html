Cosmic Questions

Cosmic Explorers is a collection of 5,000 unique algorithmically generated NFTs living on the Solana blockchain. Each Explorer has its own combination of traits and rarity levels, making them truly unique cosmic adventurers.

To mint your Cosmic Explorer, connect your Solana-compatible wallet (like Phantom) using the "Connect Wallet" button. Then select the quantity you'd like to mint (up to 2 per transaction) and click "Mint Now". Your new Explorer will be sent directly to your wallet.

Your Cosmic Explorer is your ticket to our growing universe! Holders get access to exclusive airdrops, community events, and future perks. As we roll out our roadmap, you'll be able to stake your Explorer for rewards, use it in our upcoming metaverse space station, and more.

Beyond our beautiful cosmic-themed art, we're building a comprehensive ecosystem for our holders. Our focus on community, utility, and long-term development sets us apart. Plus, our Solana-based approach means low minting costs and fast transactions compared to other chains.
